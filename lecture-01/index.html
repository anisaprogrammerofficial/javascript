Dasht E Wehshat novel by Mehwish Ali

Introduction to the Novel: “Dasht E Wehshat,” a novel by Mehwish Ali, tells the story of a gangster and a blind girl. It explores themes like love, revenge, and family betrayal. About the Author Mehwish Ali: Mehwish Ali is the writer of novel “Dasht E Wehshat”. She is a well-known author who began her career on social media before moving to various digests. She became popular among young readers for her romantic novels. Mehwish has a talent for keeping readers engaged and interested until the end. Some of her other novels include “Junoon E Ulfat”, “Tere Ishq Main Deewana Hoon Awara”, “Body Guard”, and “Intehai Junooniyat”. Noor Ul Ain and Hoor Ul Ain are twin sisters who lost their parents early and live with their uncle, who has two sons, Daad and Burhan. Burhan loves Noor and takes care of her, while Hoor, who loves Burhan, hates Noor due to past misunderstandings. They lived peacefully until Saeer Aalam entered their lives. Saeer is a fearless gangster who always gets his way. The story takes a turn when Noor, a blind girl, asks Saeer for help, and he forcefully marries her. Hoor opposes the marriage but Saeer handles her and marries Noor.
